The Journal of the ACM is a peer-reviewed scientific journal covering computer science in general, especially theoretical aspects. It is an official journal of the Association for Computing Machinery.[1] Its current editor-in-chief is Venkatesan Guruswami.

The journal was established in 1954[2] and "computer scientists universally hold the Journal of the ACM in high esteem".[3]
