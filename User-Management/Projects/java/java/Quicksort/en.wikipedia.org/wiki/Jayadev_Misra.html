Jayadev Misra

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Indian computer scientist

Jayadev Misra is an Indian computer scientist, currently the Schlumberger Centennial Chair Emeritus and Distinguished Teaching Professor Emeritus at University of Texas at Austin, and member of the IEEE and ACM.[1][2]


Awards[edit]

  • Member, National Academy of Engineering, 2018. [3]
  • Member, TAMEST (The Academy of Medicine, Engineering and Science of Texas), 2018. [4]
  • Harry H. Goode Memorial Award, IEEE, 2017 (joint with K. Mani Chandy).
  • Distinguished Alumnus Award, IIT Kanpur, 2014. [5]
  • Regents' Outstanding Teaching Award, University of Texas, 2010. [6]
  • Doctor Honoris Causa, École normale supérieure Paris-Saclay, Cachan, France, 2010. [7]
  • Distinguished Teaching Professor, UT Austin, 2009.
  • Identified as a highly cited researcher by Thomson Reuters ISI, 2004. [8]
  • ACM Fellow, 1995.[9]
  • IEEE Fellow, 1992.[10]
  • John Simon Guggenheim Fellow, 1989.
