Former pupils of The King's School, Canterbury are known in some circles as Old King's Scholars. The abbreviation OKS is sometimes used.

  • For former students of King's School, Chester, also known as "Old King's Scholars", see Category:People educated at The King's School, Chester.
  • For former students of King's College School, Wimbledon known as "Old King's", see Category:People educated at King's College School, Wimbledon
