George Jacobs is an American bridge player, who lives in Chicago. Jacobs has won 11 North American Bridge Championships.

Jacobs is a monthly columnist with the American Contract Bridge League Bulletin magazine.

Bridge accomplishments[edit]

Awards[edit]

  • Herman Trophy (2) 2000, 2001

Wins[edit]

  • North American Bridge Championships (11)
    • Lebhar IMP Pairs (1) 1999 [1]
    • Jacoby Open Swiss Teams (1) 2001 [2]
    • Vanderbilt (3) 1999, 2004, 2009 [3]
    • Mitchell Board-a-Match Teams (3) 2002, 2003, 2006 [4]
    • Reisinger (1) 2000 [5]
    • Spingold (2) 2001, 2002 [6]

Runners-up[edit]

  • Bermuda Bowl (1) 2007 [7]
  • North American Bridge Championships (6)
    • Silodor Open Pairs (1) 2005 [8]
    • Mitchell Board-a-Match Teams (1) 2001 [9]
    • Reisinger (3) 1998, 2001, 2005 [10]
    • Roth Open Swiss Teams (1) 2008 [11]
